Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your email address and an optional nickname. When you upload files, we store the file content and metadata (name, size, type, upload time) associated with your account.

2. How We Use Your Information
  • To provide and maintain the TopLinks service
  • To authenticate you when you log in
  • To associate uploaded files with your account
3. File Storage

Uploaded files are stored in Cloudflare R2 object storage. Files that you make publicly accessible via their share link can be accessed by anyone with that link.

4. Data Retention

Your account data and files are retained until you request deletion. You may contact us at any time to request removal of your data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare R2 for file storage and, optionally, Google OAuth for authentication. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Cookies

We use a session cookie solely to keep you logged in. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related requests, please contact support@toplinks.app.
